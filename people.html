A skilled team. A shared ambition.

Every project is ultimately delivered by people. At SQA, we invest in the ones who make our work possible — our engineers, site teams, commercial staff, and support functions.

Our People

Competence, character, and an upward energy.

The people of SQA are central to our ability to realise opportunities, solve project challenges, and add value for our clients. We look for competence and experience — but we also look for character, teamwork, and a willingness to keep improving.

Our teams are diverse by background, discipline, and experience. They bring engineering, architectural, commercial, safety, and operational expertise to every project — and they work together with a single focus: delivering for our clients.

What We Cultivate

The qualities that define an SQA team.

Teamwork

Collaboration across design, site, and commercial functions — without silos.

Competence

Technical depth in construction, engineering, and project management.

Character

Integrity, accountability, and respect for every colleague and partner.

Energy

Drive to solve problems quickly and find the better way to deliver.

Diversity

A broad mix of backgrounds, ages, and perspectives — making the team stronger.

Talent Development

Growing the next generation of builders.

SQA invests in people for the long term. We support our team through on-the-job mentorship, structured knowledge transfer from senior engineers, and clear pathways for career progression.

  • Structured onboarding and job exposure
  • Regular toolbox and safety briefings for all site personnel
  • Professional development support and certification sponsorship
  • Mentorship from experienced project managers and directors
  • Clear internal progression routes across commercial, engineering, and operations
