Everyone safe, every day.

Safety sits at the centre of how SQA operates. It is the foundation of Assurance — and the precondition for everything else we deliver.

“Safe projects are successful projects. All injuries are preventable.”

This principle defines how we approach every site, every shift, every task — regardless of project size, sector, or client.

Safety as Culture

Discipline, methodology, and continuous improvement.

Safety at SQA is not a binder of rules on a shelf. It is built into how our people plan, communicate, supervise, and hold each other accountable — every hour of every shift.

01

Site Safety

Daily site walk-downs, hazard identification, and immediate corrective action where risks are observed.

02

Quality Control

Inspection regimes integrated into the construction sequence — quality checks scheduled, signed off, and recorded.

03

Safe Work Practices

Method statements prepared, reviewed, and followed. Permit-to-work systems for high-risk activities.

04

Risk Management

Project-level hazard registers, regular re-assessment, and escalation pathways for emerging risks.

05

Compliance

Adherence to DOSH, OSHA, and CIDB regulations across all SQA sites and supporting documentation.

06

Worker Welfare

Site amenities, PPE, hydration, and rest provisions. Compliance enforced for direct workers and subcontractors alike.

07

Continuous Improvement

Incident learnings captured, shared across sites, and built back into method statements and toolbox talks.

08

Toolbox & Reporting

Daily toolbox briefings before shift start. Weekly safety appraisal reports reviewed by senior management.

09

Subcontractor Discipline

Clear safety expectations, induction, and audit of all subcontractors operating on SQA sites.

Safety as Assurance

Why safety is part of our brand promise.

Speed without safety is reckless. Quality without safety is hollow. Assurance is what gives clients the confidence that SQA's delivery is not only fast and well-built — but reliable, audited, and without compromise.

  • Safety as a non-negotiable component of every contract
  • Leadership accountability — visible from director level down
  • Transparent reporting to clients on site safety performance
  • Investment in PPE, site welfare, and safe working environments
  • Suitable for clients, consultants, and tender pre-qualification reviews
