Why Join Us

A career partner, not just an employer.

SQA gives capable people the projects, exposure, and culture to grow into the next stage of their career — supported by senior leaders who stay close to delivery.

Real Project Exposure

Opportunity to work on industrial, commercial, and turnkey projects across multiple sectors and project types.

Malaysian-Japanese Culture

Exposure to construction discipline shaped by both Malaysian operational strength and Japanese project management practice.

Career Growth

Hands-on project experience, structured mentorship, and clear progression pathways across engineering, commercial, and operations.

Collaborative Teams

Site, design, and office teams working as one — without silos or political overhead.

Safety & Quality

A culture where safety, quality, and professionalism are non-negotiable standards — not aspirations.

Long-Term Stability

A G7 contractor with 29 years of continuous operation and a healthy pipeline across industrial and commercial sectors.
