Build your career with a dynamic, growing contractor.

SQA is always looking for capable people with the drive to deliver — across construction, design, engineering, commercial, and support functions.

Why Join SQA

We are looking for capable professionals who share our commitment to delivery, quality, and safety.

As a progressive Malaysian-Japanese contractor with a strong pipeline of turnkey projects, SQA offers an operating environment that combines challenging work with structured project management discipline. We invest in our people, and we recognise the individuals who help us build our reputation.

Life at SQA

Our people, our culture, our community.

Beyond the projects, SQA is a team that invests in its people — through team events, community sponsorships, safety culture, and continuous learning.

Annual Company Gathering Bringing together teams from across all active project sites.
Community Sponsorships Supporting local initiatives and education programmes.
Safety Campaigns Monthly safety awareness drives on every site.
Training & Development Structured learning across engineering and commercial functions.
Site Team Events Celebrating project milestones and team achievements.

What We Offer

Hiring across every part of the business.

Our open roles span site operations, design, engineering, commercial, and corporate functions — reflecting the full scope of a turnkey contractor.

Construction

Project managers, site engineers, supervisors, foremen, and project coordinators for active sites.

Architecture & Engineering

In-house architects, structural engineers, and design coordinators supporting turnkey delivery.

Mechanical & Electrical

M&E engineers for design coordination, site supervision, and commissioning.

Quantity Survey

QS professionals for tendering, contract administration, variation management, and cost reporting.

Business Development

Commercial and client-facing roles supporting tender pipeline and strategic growth.

Corporate & Support

Finance, human resources, administration, procurement, logistics, and renovation / maintenance roles.
