A progressive Malaysian-Japanese contractor with a quarter-century of disciplined delivery.

SQA Builders Sdn Bhd is part of the SQA Group, established in 1997. The name "SQA" reflects our three guiding principles: Speed, Quality and Assurance.

Who We Are

SQA Builders is a respected turnkey contractor providing a wide range of construction services and solutions to a growing base of discerning clients across Malaysia.

Founded on the combined strengths of Malaysian construction experience and Japanese project management discipline, we have built a reputable track record delivering commercial offices, showrooms, factory warehouses, bungalows, condominiums, and institutional buildings. Our approach is practical, operational, and grounded in the commercial realities of our clients' businesses.

Today, SQA is registered with the Construction Industry Development Board (CIDB) of Malaysia at Grade G7 — the highest available grade — qualifying us to undertake construction works of unlimited value in the private and public sectors.

Our Track Record

Delivering across sectors, at scale.

Over more than two decades, SQA Builders has accumulated delivery experience across most major building categories in Malaysia. Our portfolio includes:

  • Commercial offices and corporate headquarters
  • Showrooms and branded retail spaces, including flagship automotive outlets
  • Factory warehouses and logistics facilities
  • Cold storage and food processing plants
  • Bungalows and luxury residential projects
  • Condominium and staged residential developments
  • Institutional buildings including schools, academies, and embassies
