Who We Are

SQA is a leading Japanese-Malaysian design and build construction company, specialising in industrial property construction with a strong focus on logistics and warehouse solutions. Since 1997, we have combined the assuredness of Japanese engineering with deep local expertise in Malaysia to deliver high-performance, future-ready facilities tailored to operational needs.

Our philosophy is rooted in sustainable, environmentally responsible construction, guided by ethical practices and a steadfast commitment to occupational health and safety. For us, sustainability is not just an aspiration — it is a responsibility.

Today, SQA is registered with the Construction Industry Development Board (CIDB) of Malaysia at Grade G7 — the highest available grade — qualifying us to undertake construction works of unlimited value in the private and public sectors.
