Our Story

Building a legacy of excellence since 2010.

CK Construction was founded with a simple mission: to deliver construction projects of the highest quality while maintaining the trust and safety of our clients and communities.

Over the past 15 years, we have grown from a small local contractor into a full-service construction firm, delivering residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects across the region.

Our success is built on a foundation of integrity, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to craftsmanship. Every project we undertake reflects our dedication to turning visions into lasting structures.
