About SQA

SQA Builders is a progressive Malaysian-Japanese construction company providing a wide range of construction services and solutions to a growing base of discerning clients.

The name "SQA" stands for Speed, Quality and Assurance — the three principles that define how we approach every project, from initial design coordination to final handover. Respected as a turnkey contractor, SQA has built a reputation for delivering construction, project, and design management services that are practical, efficient, and commercially sound.

Our team combines Malaysian construction experience with Japanese project management discipline, resulting in shorter project periods, tighter control of quality, and a culture of safety that runs through every site we operate.

01 — Speed

Built with Speed

Shorter project periods through turnkey delivery, disciplined coordination, and proven building systems including IBS. We protect your commercial timelines.

02 — Quality

Delivered with Quality

Workmanship backed by experience across industrial, commercial, institutional, and residential works — and the standards expected by Japanese corporate clients.

Assured by Safety

Why Clients Choose SQA

A contractor built around client outcomes.

Clients choose SQA for the combination of turnkey capability, disciplined project management, and a hands-on approach to site operations. The result: fewer interfaces, fewer surprises, and projects that finish on time and on budget.

Turnkey Capability

Single point of responsibility across design, procurement, and construction — reducing coordination risk and accelerating decisions.

Design & Build Integration

Our design and construction teams work as one — resolving buildability, cost, and schedule issues early, not on site.

Shorter Project Periods

Industrial Building Systems (IBS), prefabrication, and tight site sequencing consistently deliver faster completion.

Disciplined Coordination

Japanese-style Project Management Methodology with structured reporting, follow-up, and issue resolution at every level.

Cost & Time Efficiency

Direct subcontractor management and accurate early-stage cost planning give clients visibility and control.

Safety-First Culture

Safety is not a checklist — it is part of Assurance. Weekly appraisal boards, toolbox meetings, and continuous training keep every site accountable.
