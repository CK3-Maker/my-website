SQA Builders Sdn Bhd

A Leading Industrial Contractor in Malaysia.

SQA Builders Sdn Bhd, part of the SQA Group established in 1997, is a progressive Malaysian-Japanese contractor built on the principles of Speed, Quality, and Assurance. With nearly three decades of construction experience, SQA delivers integrated construction solutions for industrial, commercial, institutional, and residential developments.

Our strength lies in our ability to combine Japanese discipline, Malaysian market knowledge, and practical project execution. From design and build to turnkey delivery, MEP coordination, value engineering, and development partnerships, SQA supports clients from early planning through to successful project completion.

Over the years, SQA has earned a strong reputation as a reliable turnkey contractor — delivering high-quality offices, showrooms, production plants, warehouses, bungalows, condominiums, and institutional buildings. We work closely with local and international clients to help them achieve their commercial goals through efficient, dependable, and professionally managed construction solutions.
