Co-development and joint-venture opportunities.

Beyond contracting, SQA is actively engaging in property development — pursuing selective joint-venture arrangements with landowners and development partners.

Development Ambition

SQA is broadening its business beyond contracting, taking selective positions in property development through co-development and joint-venture arrangements.

Backed by more than 25 years of construction delivery experience and a disciplined commercial approach, we are well-positioned to partner with landowners who wish to realise the development potential of their sites — without taking on the full execution and capital burden alone.

Target Development Types

The projects we are pursuing.

We focus on development formats where our construction capability, turnkey discipline, and IBS experience provide clear commercial advantage.

Shop-Office Developments

Mixed-use shop-office schemes in commercial catchments — delivering retail frontage with office or residential components above.

Industrial Developments

Purpose-built warehouses, logistics hubs, and factory facilities — leveraging SQA's track record in large-span IBS industrial construction for end-user tenants or investor portfolios.

Residential Projects

Landed and stratified residential developments — from boutique bungalow clusters to mid-density condominium projects.

For Landowners & Partners

What an SQA partnership looks like.

We bring more than construction to a joint-venture table. Our capability set is designed to simplify project execution for landowners who want a capable, hands-on partner.

  • End-to-end construction capability under a G7 licence
  • Experienced design-and-build team reducing consultant overhead
  • Project management discipline drawn from Japanese practice
  • Access to subcontractor and supply relationships built over 25+ years
  • Structured approach to feasibility, phasing, and cost control
  • Transparent commercial structuring and reporting
Design & Build

Integrated Design & Build capability for development partners.

SQA's Property Development offering is backed by an in-house Design & Build capability — allowing us to take a project from concept master-planning through to handover under a single, accountable contract. This is particularly valuable for landowners who want an all-in-one partner, not a consortium to manage.

Concept & Feasibility

Highest-and-best-use analysis, preliminary massing, development yield studies, and indicative cost plans to support landowner decision-making.

Architectural Design

In-house architectural design with coordination across authorities, consultants, and downstream construction teams — compressing timelines and reducing friction.

Engineering Coordination

Structural and M&E design coordinated with our construction team from day one — improving buildability and preventing costly late-stage changes.

Cost Planning & Control

Target-cost planning from concept stage, with continuous value engineering and transparent reporting throughout delivery.

IBS & Buildability

Early integration of Industrialised Building Systems (IBS) where appropriate — accelerating delivery and improving predictability of both cost and quality.

Construction & Handover

Full construction delivery under SQA's G7 licence — with the same safety culture and disciplined project management applied to our main contracting business.

Why this matters for landowners

A Design & Build partner removes the need to appoint and coordinate separate architects, engineers, QS firms, and contractors. For landowners evaluating a JV or co-development, this means fewer interfaces, shorter approval cycles, and a single, accountable party responsible for delivering the asset as intended.

Own land with development potential?

We welcome conversations with landowners, family offices, and strategic partners who are considering development options for their sites. Initial discussions are confidential and without obligation.
