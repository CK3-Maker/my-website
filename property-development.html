Open to Opportunities. Built for Long-Term Value.

SQA is open to opportunities across industrial, commercial, and residential property development.

Focus Areas

Three sectors where we are actively exploring opportunities.

Industrial Development

From semi-detached industrial units to larger build-to-suit facilities, warehouses, factories, logistics facilities, and custom industrial assets, SQA is open to practical industrial development opportunities.

Commercial Shoplots

SQA is open to strategic commercial shoplot developments in pocket areas with practical market potential and strong accessibility.

Residential Development

From smaller standalone developments to larger residential schemes and township opportunities, SQA is open to exploring residential development potential with suitable partners and landowners.

Development Models

Two ways to engage with us.

Joint Venture Model

SQA is open to working with landowners and strategic partners through joint venture structures where development potential, construction expertise, and long-term value creation can be aligned.

Land Proposal Model

Landowners with suitable land parcels may submit proposals for SQA's review. We will assess each opportunity based on location, land use potential, accessibility, market demand, development feasibility, and strategic fit.

Have a Land or Development Opportunity?

Share your proposal with SQA and our team will review the opportunity based on location, feasibility, market potential, and strategic fit.
