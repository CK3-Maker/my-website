Construction, project, and design management — delivered as one capability.

SQA has built its reputation on delivering integrated construction services. Our capabilities combine technical competence, commercial discipline, and a structured project management approach inherited from our Japanese heritage.

What We Do

Since 1997, SQA has delivered construction, project, and design management services for clients across industrial, commercial, residential, and institutional sectors.

We operate across three main delivery models — construction contracting, turnkey design-and-build, and major renovation and extension works — and we apply the same discipline and quality control regardless of project scale or sector.

Delivery Models

Three ways SQA delivers for clients.

Construction Contracting

Traditional main contractor services — executing designs provided by the client's consultants with the discipline expected of a G7 contractor.

Turnkey Builder

Integrated design-and-build delivery — a single contract covering design, procurement, and construction for faster, simpler project execution.

Major Renovation & Extension

Extensions, structural refits, and major refurbishments — delivered without disrupting ongoing client operations where required.

Turnkey Advantage

Why our clients choose turnkey delivery.

For many clients — particularly corporate and industrial principals — the turnkey model is the most efficient route to a completed facility. SQA takes full responsibility for design, procurement, and construction under a single agreement.

  • Integrated design and build — fewer interfaces to manage
  • Reduced stress and coordination burden for the client
  • Shorter overall project periods
  • Direct subcontractor coordination without intermediaries
  • Clearer accountability for cost, time, and quality
  • Greater flexibility during construction to absorb changes
Project Management Methodology

Japanese-inspired project discipline, internalised across SQA.

Our internal Project Management Methodology (PMM) draws on the structure and follow-through culture of Japanese construction management. It shapes how we monitor performance, align teams, and resolve issues at every stage of delivery.

01 — MONITOR

Performance Tracking

Continuous monitoring of project progress against baseline schedule, cost, and quality metrics.

02 — ALIGN

Strategic Alignment

Keeping project teams, subcontractors, and client representatives aligned on priorities and sequence.

03 — RESOLVE

Prompt Issue Resolution

Issues are raised, owned, and closed — with structured reporting and disciplined follow-up.

04 — REPORT

Detailed Reporting

Transparent, detailed reporting to clients — no surprises, no hidden issues.

Renovation & Maintenance

Full-service renovation and maintenance works.

SQA's renovation and maintenance capability complements our main contracting business. We handle everything from structural extensions to interior refurbishments — with the same discipline and safety culture.

  • Major extension and expansion works
  • Demolition works and site preparation
  • Construction and structural works
  • Mechanical and electrical works
  • Refurbishment and interior fit-out
  • Planned preventive maintenance
