Home / About Us / Vision & Mission

Why we build — and how we build it.

SQA's vision sets the direction for the company. Our mission defines how we operate every day. Together, they guide how we serve our clients, develop our people, and approach every project.

Our Vision

To be the contractor of choice for clients who value Speed, Quality, and Assurance.

We aspire to build a reputation — project by project — as the progressive Malaysian-Japanese contractor clients turn to when delivery certainty matters. Our vision is anchored in three commitments:

  • To set the benchmark in turnkey project delivery across industrial, commercial, residential, and institutional sectors
  • To grow a capable, motivated team that represents the best of Malaysian and Japanese construction practice
  • To remain a company our clients trust, our people are proud of, and our partners rely on
Our Mission

A practical mission, focused on delivery.

We are committed to constantly improving our project delivery and operational efficiency, so that our clients can meet their commercial goals with confidence — and so that our people are equipped, empowered, and proud of the work we produce together.

We believe that a contractor is only as good as the reliability of its delivery. That is why we invest continuously in our systems, our methodologies, and our people — to ensure that every project SQA undertakes is completed safely, on time, within budget, and to a standard that reflects well on our clients and ourselves.

Our Values

Five principles we hold ourselves to.

These values are not aspirational — they are the working standards of our company and the expectations we carry into every project.

Fairness

Balanced, honest dealings with clients, consultants, subcontractors, and colleagues.

Passion

Genuine pride in construction as a craft, and in the finished work we hand over to our clients.

Experience

Over two decades of accumulated know-how across most major Malaysian building sectors.

Innovation

Continuous improvement in methods, systems, and building technologies such as IBS.

Relationships

Long-term partnerships with clients, consultants, and trade partners — repeat work is our best reference.

Built on principles. Proven on site.

The best test of any mission statement is the quality of work produced in its name. We invite you to review our project portfolio.
