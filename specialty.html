Technical strengths that differentiate SQA.

Beyond general contracting, SQA is recognised for specific technical capabilities — most notably our application of Industrialised Building Systems (IBS) and our expertise in large-span industrial construction.

IBS — Industrialised Building System

A construction technology that reshapes delivery.

Industrialised Building Systems (IBS) is one of SQA's signature capabilities. By prefabricating building components under factory-controlled conditions and assembling them on site, we deliver buildings faster, cleaner, and with more consistent quality outcomes.

Our IBS capability has been applied to industrial facilities, commercial buildings, and residential projects — and is especially suited to projects where speed of completion and predictable quality are critical.

IBS Benefits

Why IBS matters to our clients.

Reduced Dependence on Unskilled Labour

Prefabricated components reduce on-site labour intensity, mitigating the impact of labour shortages and variability in workmanship.

Reduced Wastage

Factory production minimises construction waste — better for cost, better for sustainability, and better for site tidiness.

Better Quality Control

Components produced in controlled factory conditions deliver more consistent dimensional accuracy, finishes, and performance.
