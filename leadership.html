Experienced leadership. Operational focus.

SQA's leadership brings together architectural, engineering, construction, and commercial expertise — shaped by decades of practice in both Malaysian and Japanese operating environments.

Board of Directors

The board directing our company.

Our Board provides strategic direction, governance oversight, and long-term stewardship of the SQA Group — while remaining hands-on with major project and client relationships.

Key Management

The senior managers running our projects.

Our key management team leads day-to-day operations across engineering, commercial, safety, and support functions.

Leadership Philosophy

Hands-on involvement. Long-term client relationships.

At SQA, senior management remain actively engaged with every project — from early-stage contract negotiation through to site visits and post-completion follow-up. This hands-on culture is a deliberate choice.

  • Direct client engagement at principal-to-principal level
  • Weekly project reviews at senior management level
  • Commitment to accurate reporting and transparent communication
  • Investment in the development of the next generation of site and commercial leaders
